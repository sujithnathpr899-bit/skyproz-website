Safety systems

Every Job Begins with a Safer Plan

Our safety framework is designed to protect people, assets, and operations before the first rope is rigged.

01

Risk Assessment

Identify hazards, site constraints, access risks, and operational requirements before mobilisation.

02

Method Statements

Prepare practical work methods, equipment plans, rescue arrangements, and control measures.

03

Emergency Rescue Planning

Define rescue procedures, communication flow, standby readiness, and escalation paths.

04

PPE Compliance

Use appropriate helmets, harnesses, ropes, connectors, fall protection, and site-specific PPE.

05

Quality Control

Inspect workmanship, record completion evidence, and support client handover requirements.

06

Working at Height Procedures

Execute rope access and height work with disciplined supervision and approved procedures.
